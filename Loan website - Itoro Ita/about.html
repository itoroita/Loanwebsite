About Us

We strive to understand our customers and leverage technology, data and innovation to deliver outstanding service experiences and we are Nigeria’s most convenient lending company – delivering outstanding service experiences.

Credit Financials has two investment products: Fixed Deposit - A tenured investment with up to 19% on a minimum of N200, 000 deposit fixed for 30 days to 360 days Target Savings - A high yield savings account with 10%p.a. interest on a minimum of N20,000 monthly savings. For Target Savings, you can add any amount at any time to your account. For Fixed Deposit, we will re-invest the sum of your old and new investment for the tenure you choose.

What We Do

Credit Financials is a fintech lending company operating under a microfinance banking license in Lagos, Nigeria. We provide loans to individuals and small businesses through our website, contact center, agent network and branches. Regulated by the CBN and insured by the NDIC, we also offer market leading rates on Fixed Deposits and Savings accounts.
